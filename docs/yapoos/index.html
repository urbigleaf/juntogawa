YAPOOS

YAPOOS promoting “Men’s Junan” (1991)

YAPOOS (ヤプーズ) is a Japanese new wave and rock band that started in 1983 as Togawa Jun’s backing band until Togawa joined and made them into an official band. They are noted for their disturbing lyrics which has connected them in many ways to the ero-guro subculture. The band’s name is taken from a book.

YAPOOS promoting “Barbara Sexeroid” (1987)

YAPOOS in Player (1988)

Members

  • Togawa Jun (戸川純; Vocals)
  • Nakahara Nobuo (中原信雄; Bass and Synthesizer)
  • Lion Merry (ライオン・メリイ; Keyboard)
  • Norihiro “BERA” Ishizuka (石塚”BERA”伯広; Guitar)
  • Atsunobu Yakabe (矢壁アツノブ 矢壁 篤信; Drums)

  • Yoshikawa Youichiro (吉川洋一郎; Synthesizer from 1985-1991)
